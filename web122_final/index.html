Hospice International

Since 1983, Hospice Institute has been dedicated to providing compassionate, comprehensive care for patient with life-limiting or life-threatening conditions. We support not only the medical needs of our patients, but also the emotional, psychological, and spiritual well-being of their families. From diagnosis through every stage of care. Our mission is to ensure that no child faces serious illness alone, and that every family receives the comfort, dignity, and hope they deserve.

Our Programs & Actions

We are committed to improving the quality of life for children with life-limiting and life-threatening conditions by focusing on more than just medical care. Through comprehensive education, compassionate advocacy, and strong global partnerships, we empower caregivers, families, and healthcare professionals with the knowledge, tools, and support they need. Our goal is to ensure that every child, regardless of geography or circumstance, has access to comfort, dignity, and a continuum of care that respects their unique journey and strengthens their family’s resilience.

Education & Training

We offer workshops, online resources, and personalized support for medical professionals and caregivers around the world.

Advocacy & Policy

We work with organizations and governments to ensure children’s palliative care is recognized, funded, and integrated into health systems.

Global Collaboration

We connect with international partners to expand access to compassionate care for all children, regardless of location or background.
