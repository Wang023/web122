Who We Are

Caring Hospice Institute (CHI) is a dedicated provider of compassionate and dignified hospice care. Rooted in the belief that every individual deserves comfort, respect, and quality care in life’s final stages, we offer holistic services that address the physical, emotional, and spiritual well-being of both patients and their loved ones. Headquartered in Los Angeles, CHI is a trusted partner for families navigating end-of-life care.

Our Mission

At CHI, our mission is simple yet profound: to provide exceptional hospice care that enhances the quality of life for individuals with life-limiting illnesses. We strive to ensure that every patient experiences dignity, peace, and compassionate support during this critical time. Our approach integrates medical expertise with emotional and spiritual care, allowing families to focus on what matters most—spending meaningful time together.

Our Team

CHI is comprised of a highly skilled and compassionate team, all dedicated to delivering personalized care. We believe in treating every patient with dignity and respect, ensuring that they receive the support they need in the comfort of their chosen setting—whether at home, in a care facility, or in one of our partner locations.

Our Commitment to Excellence

As a leading provider of hospice care in California, we are committed to advancing care standards and fostering a supportive community for those in need. Our team continually seeks innovative ways to improve the hospice experience through education, advocacy, and compassionate service.
